Welcome to Tender Living Home!

A modern, state-licensed, wheelchair-accessible Adult Foster Care home in Kalamazoo, Tender Living Home is where safety, dignity, and comfort come first. Located in a quiet, accessible neighborhood just minutes from downtown, our newly renovated residence offers all-private rooms in a warm, home-like setting.

We care for adults who need extra support while maintaining their independence—with compassion, respect, and heart.

Explore our home, meet our team, and learn more about the personalized care that makes Tender Living Home feel like family.

About Us

Personalized Care. Professional Standards. A Place to Call Home.

At Tender Living Home, we believe everyone deserves to age with dignity in a setting that promotes well-being, connection, and peace of mind. As a state-licensed and wheelchair-accessible Adult Foster Care (AFC) home, we meet and exceed the standards of care required by the State of Michigan.

Our home was thoughtfully renovated to meet the evolving needs of our residents—offering a modern yet comfortable environment where individuals can thrive. With a dedicated team of experienced caregivers, we provide hands-on support while honoring each resident’s independence, preferences, and unique story.

Tender Living Home is more than a place to live. It’s a place to belong.


Where Modern Living Meets Meaningful Care

Tender Living Home was designed with both comfort and functionality in mind. Every detail of our home supports a lifestyle of dignity, community, and ease.

  • All Private Bedrooms
    Spacious and tastefully furnished, with room for personal items.

  • Newly Renovated Spaces
    Modern kitchen, cozy common areas, and accessible bathrooms.

  • Fully Wheelchair-Accessible Design
    Single-level layout, wide doorways, grab bars, and barrier-free entrances for safety and mobility.

  • Relaxation Areas
    Enjoy fresh air and nature in our secure porch.

Located in a peaceful, well-connected Kalamazoo neighborhood, residents enjoy proximity to parks, clinics, shops, and downtown.


We provide 24/7 care tailored to the physical, emotional, and social needs of each resident. Our services include:

  • Personal Care
    Assistance Help with bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and mobility..

  • Newly Renovated Spaces
    Safe and timely administration and documentation of all medications.

  • Nutritious Home-Cooked Meals
    Delicious, balanced meals with attention to dietary needs and preferences.

  • Housekeeping & Laundry
    Regular cleaning and linen services to keep living spaces fresh and comfortable.

  • Activities & Engagement
    Daily social and recreational activities to encourage connection, creativity, and joy.

  • Coordination of Care
    Communication with family members, case managers, and healthcare providers to ensure seamless care.
