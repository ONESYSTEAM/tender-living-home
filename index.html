THANK YOU FOR BEING HERE.

We’re a modern, state-licensed, wheelchair-accessible Adult Foster Care home nestled in the beautiful Millwood neighborhood of Kalamazoo. Just minutes from downtown, our quiet, accessible location offers all-private rooms in a newly renovated, home-like setting where safety, dignity, and comfort come first.

But Tender Living Home is more than just a place to live—it’s a place to belong. We care for adults who need extra support while maintaining their independence, delivering personalized care with compassion, respect, and heart.

Explore our home, meet our team, and discover what makes Tender Living Home feel like family. Here’s to new beginnings and meaningful connections.
